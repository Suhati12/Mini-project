Featured Artists

Leonardo da Vinci

1452-1519

Italian Renaissance artist, inventor, and scientist. Famous for the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper.

Vincent van Gogh

1853-1890

Dutch painter known for bold colors and emotional style. Created Starry Night and many self-portraits.

Frida Kahlo

1907-1954

Mexican artist known for self-portraits and works inspired by Mexican culture.

Pablo Picasso

1881-1973

Spanish painter who co-founded Cubism. One of the most influential artists of the 20th century.

Art Movement Timeline

1400-1600

Renaissance

Revival of classical learning and wisdom, emphasis on humanism and realistic representation.

1600-1750

Baroque

Dramatic use of light and shadow, emotional intensity, and grandeur in art and architecture.

1860-1886

Impressionism

Focus on light and its changing qualities, ordinary subject matter, and visible brush strokes.

1907-1914

Cubism

Revolutionary approach showing subjects from multiple viewpoints, geometric forms and fragmentation.

1920-1940

Surrealism

Exploration of the unconscious mind, dreams, and the irrational through art and literature.
